Welcome to Hope Family Therapy and Consultation Services

Our Purpose

Restoring hope by providing culturally competent and client-centered services to individuals with mental health problems and helping them to live their best lives. We are Medicare and NJ Medicaid Approved Provider.

mission

Our Services

Here at Hope Family Therapy and Consultation Services, we provide quality and compassionate mental health care to our patients. These include;

*Trauma-Focused Cognitive Behavior Therapy (TF-CBT)
*Post-Traumatic Stress Disorders (PTSD)
*Grief/Loss
*Cognitive Behavior Therapy (CBT)
*Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
*Attention-Deficits Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
*Oppositional Defiant Disorder (ODD)
*Anxiety Management
*Anger Management
*Communication
*Life skills development
*Emotional dysregulation
*DD/ID services
*Social skills
*Parenting skills
*Family Conflict
*Couples Therapy
*Individual Therapy for Relationship Issues
